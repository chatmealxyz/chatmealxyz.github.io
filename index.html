What is Chatmeal?
Chatmeal is a Whatsapp-powered food ordering tool for restaurants. It is built together with multiple restaurant owners and is  designed to solve following problems:
20% commission is too much
Delivery apps like Gojek, Grab, Swiggy or Uber Eats on average charge a 20% commission. As a result, restaurant owners have 3 options: to increase the prices, to cut the cost by giving less food, or to be left with 0 margins.
Too many tools
You wanted just to serve tasty food and too make people happy, right? Nobody warned you that you will have to become an IT expert. Table booking system, POS system, Website builders, Linktree builders, Canva or Photoshop for menu. There is no end to that list. Too. Many. Tools.
You don't know your customer
When an order from delivery app comes, you only talk to a courier. Did you customers enjoy your food or not? Are they going to order again?  Who are they, and how can you make them happier? You have no idea.
